About

I am a Senior Researcher at the School of Informatics, University of Edinburgh. I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the National University of Singapore (2020–2024), where I was affiliated with the Centre for Quantum Technologies (CQT), with a thesis on circuit-based quantum algorithms.

My research interests span quantum property testing, quantum learning theory, quantum computational complexity, quantum computational cryptography, and AI for quantum.
